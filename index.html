history of football

Football has a long history, with alternative versions of the game existing much earlier than the current form of the game1. The original game was revolutionized in 1863 with the formation of the English Football Association and the publication of the first official rules2. These basic rules may have changed but their ideals are very similar to today, and have helped the development of football, which has been rapidly growing ever since2.

origin of football

Modern football originated in Britain in the 19th century1234. Before that, “folk football” games were played in towns and villages according to local customs and with minimal rules. The game began to be standardized when it was taken up as a winter game at public schools12.
